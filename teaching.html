Teaching philosophy

I believe students learn best when they can immediately connect theory to lived economic challenges. Each session blends narrative, computation, and collaborative problem-solving so students graduate with both analytical rigour and practical instincts.

  • Applied first: Labs launch with policy datasets before we formalise the model.
  • Evidence-based: We use emerging research, including my own projects, to spark debate.
  • Inclusive: Classroom rituals ensure everyone contributes, learns, and feels heard.
