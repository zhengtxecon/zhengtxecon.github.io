Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly do you respond to emails?

I typically respond to emails within 48 hours during weekdays. For urgent matters, please include "Urgent" in the subject line.

Can I collaborate on research projects?

I'm always open to research collaborations, especially in my areas of interest. Please send me a brief proposal including your research interests and how they might align with my work.

Do you supervise graduate students?

Yes, I supervise MSc and occasionally PhD students working in areas related to my research. Please contact me with your research proposal and we can discuss potential supervision.

How do I join the Applied Economics Reading Group?

The Applied Economics Reading Group is open to all interested students and faculty. Please email me to be added to the mailing list for upcoming meetings and paper discussions.
