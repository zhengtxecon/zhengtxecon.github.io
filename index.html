I'm a 3rd-year PhD in Economics at UCL, focusing on how technology innovation and adoption shape organizational structures and individual decision-making processes, particularly in developing countries.

I also explore topics in urban economics and how behavioral heuristics and social norms influence inequality and societal biases. Currently, I co-organize the Applied Economics Reading Group at UCL.
